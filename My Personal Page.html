I took the Similar Minds personality test to see what type of personality I have, and the results were surprisingly accurate:

In my opinion, the Similar Minds test holds some general validity. In the screenshot above, it shows many different percentages of common personality traits of people in modern times. In my case, many of these perfectly describe who I am as a person. Let's take being easygoing for example. According to the Similar Minds test, being easygoing means being compelled to relax, take it easy, not to get worked up about most things, prefers peace and leisure, and dislikes conflict. I have never been the type of person to seek out problems with other people in my time on Earth, and I'll also jump at the first chance I get to relax as doing work 24/7 is honestly too much stress. Another example that I can agree with, regrettably, is with the borderline personality disorder observation. It says individuals with this disorder have issues regulating their emotions, and I've always had problems with my anger and getting irritated too easily. This test also says that I'm a type 9 personality, which consists of the healthy type: "The Stoic", the average type: "The Monk", and the unhealthy type: "The Slacker". Personally, I believe all 3 types apply to me perfectly; I always aim to put people around me at ease, i'm unpretentious, easy-going, and peaceful, but on the negative side, I can be lazy, passive, and sometimes overly compliant.
